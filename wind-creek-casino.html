Wind Creek Casino – From Local Slot House to Full‑Scale Resort

Evolving a small slot house into a modern resort destination designed to compete with major markets.

Overview

When PCI Gaming in Atmore, Alabama, set out to open Wind Creek Casino, they were making a bold leap: evolving from a small slot house into a modern resort designed to compete with destinations like Biloxi. This required naming, branding, and launch materials that reflected the sophistication of the property.

My Role

  • Helped develop names and logos for all restaurants, lounges, and retail outlets.
  • Produced the entire range of guest-facing collateral, ensuring a consistent brand identity across signage, dining, retail, and hospitality.
  • Guided the creative execution to unify the property under a cohesive, modern identity.

Impact

The launch elevated Wind Creek from a local operation into a regional destination and began a 13‑year partnership—growing from a 1,000‑player database into a multi‑property portfolio with 8 casinos and hundreds of thousands of loyalty members.
