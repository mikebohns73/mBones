Winzlow Nation: Interactive World for a Children’s Book Series

A digital universe that expands a children’s book series into an engaging, interactive world for kids and families.

Overview

Winzlow Nation is a digital universe created to expand a children’s book series into an engaging, interactive online world. Our goal was to capture the energy, imagination, and culture of the author’s stories, bring them online, and make them accessible to kids and families everywhere.

My Role

  • Partnered directly with the author and investors to define creative direction and ensure alignment with brand vision and business goals.
  • Oversaw concept development, UX/UI design, and creative execution across the site.
  • Collaborated with developers to integrate advanced interactive features, ensuring a seamless user experience.

Key Features

  • Interactive Map: Parallax-based exploration that reveals characters and stories region by region.
  • Digital Passport: Visitors collected stamps for each location explored, reinforcing progress and adventure.
  • Learning & Culture: Characters introduced children to their unique backgrounds, sparking curiosity.
  • Activities & Games: Downloadable printouts, puzzles, and activities that extend engagement beyond the screen.
  • E‑commerce Store: Integrated shop for books, merchandise, and collectibles to grow revenue and reach.

Impact

A colorful, immersive platform that bridged storytelling and interactivity—encouraging exploration, repeat visits, and deeper engagement with the world and characters.
