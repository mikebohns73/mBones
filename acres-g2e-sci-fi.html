Acres Manufacturing: G2E Sci-Fi Signage That Stole the Show

Overview

For the Global Gaming Expo in Las Vegas, Acres Manufacturing needed to stand out among industry giants. We created a bold sci‑fi themed campaign that transformed their booth and made Acres impossible to miss.

My Role

  • Developed messaging and oversaw booth graphics, signage, product demos, and handouts.
  • Produced a 60′ × 30′ banner that dominated the expo floor.
  • Developed a fun, futuristic theme that positioned Acres as an innovator.

Impact

The creative theme caught the attention of the Las Vegas Review‑Journal, which shifted coverage to include Acres alongside much larger gaming companies. The campaign boosted Acres’ visibility, positioning them as a forward‑thinking brand ready to compete at the highest level.
